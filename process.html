Why choose us ?

What is medical illustration ?
Broadly, it's the application of visual arts to health communications. We create illustrations, animations and 3D models to show the invisible and clarify the complex.
About Devon Medical Art
DMA's artwork has appeared in surgical textbooks, heath education websites, conference presentations, patient education films, journals, and iPhone® Apps. Subjects have included head and neck anatomy, facial plastic surgery, breast reconstruction, biomedical devices, pharmaceutical MOA, novel transplant surgery and cancer staging.
